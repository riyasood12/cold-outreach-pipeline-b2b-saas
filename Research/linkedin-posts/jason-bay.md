Date: October 10, 2023

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_sales-prospecting-outbound-activity-7117479576535977985-a8VY

Summary: Introduces "boring subject lines" for achieving 40%+ open rates, avoid me-centric or product-centric subject lines and mirror the prospect's internal communication style instead; provides 5 subject line frameworks each with a good vs. bad example, all under 5 words and problem/trigger-focused.

## Post 2
Date: August 28, 2023

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_sales-prospecting-outbound-activity-7101897351732056064-QbI3

Summary: A 14-point anti-pattern checklist for outbound motions: messaging written in silos by marketing or senior execs, no investment in clean data, no engagement platform for AEs, no recorded calls, no playbook, and front-line leaders who never make cold calls alongside reps.

Key Takeaway: Outbound fails at the system level, no single tactic fix compensates for an org that hasn't invested in data, tooling, rep enablement, and leader participation simultaneously.

Category: SDR Workflow / Outbound Org Design QuickMail

## Post 3
Date: December 21, 2023

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_sales-prospecting-outbound-activity-7143585908737404928-TgVi

Summary: An 8-point case study of how an AE landed 3 new opportunities in one day: dedicated 1 daily outbound hour, cut email copy from 150 to 75 words, reframed messaging from features to business impact, collaborated with SDRs, created 2-3 reusable personalization templates, A/B tested, narrowed to top two personas and industries, and maintained 12-15 touches per prospect.

Key Takeaway: AE outbound success compounds from small disciplined choices — ICP narrowing, copy brevity, and SDR collaboration not any single tactic or template.

Category: Cold Email / AE Self-Sourcing / Outbound Workflow SalesBread

## Post 5
Date: October 12, 2023

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasondbay_sales-prospecting-outbound-activity-7118206306963832833-XttI

Summary: Shares a signal-driven account research framework: Sales Navigator boolean searches (past employees at client companies, newly hired executives, hiring signals, TeamLink), Seeking Alpha for public company earnings transcripts, and Boolean site searches all feeding a tiered account list before any outreach begins.

Key Takeaway: The best outbound starts before the first message — signal-driven account prioritisation converts meetings at dramatically higher rates than working a list from the top down.

Category: Account Research / Prospecting Strategy / SDR Workflow 30mpc

## Cross-Cutting Themes

System over tactic — every post frames outbound as a compounding infrastructure (ICP + data + messaging + enablement + leader participation) — individual tactics fail without the surrounding system
Prospect-first language across every channel - subject lines, email openers, and call hooks all share one principle: lead with what you know about them, not who you are or what you sell
Quality contact strategy beats volume - the 12-15 touch cadence, signal-driven account research, and quality-first cold calling framework all share the same belief: fewer, better-researched prospects outperform spray-and-pray at every conversion stage
