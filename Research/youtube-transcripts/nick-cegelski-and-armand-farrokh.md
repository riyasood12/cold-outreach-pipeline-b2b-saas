# 30MPC: Nick Cegelski + Armand Farrokh

30MPC: 2 Most Valuable YouTube Videos on Modern B2B SaaS Outbound Systems
The two most valuable videos for understanding modern B2B SaaS outbound systems from the 30MPC lens are Cold Call Masterclass: The Perfect Script (With LIVE Calls to Prove It) and Cold Calling Masterclass: 52 Minutes of Proven Cold Calling Tips (For B2B Sales) because they cover the core mechanics of modern outbound execution: opening, problem-led messaging, objection handling, meeting conversion, and rep workflow.

1) Cold Call Masterclass: The Perfect Script (With LIVE Calls to Prove It)

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2vivv2HeiBU
 
Date: Jan 20, 2026 

Transcript Summary
- Armand Farrokh and Nick Cegelski argue that cold call openers must break the telemarketer stigma by leading with context instead of generic permission-based lines.

- They present two preferred opener styles: the “heard the name tossed around” opener and the tailored permission opener, both designed to sound familiar and specific.

- The pitch framework centers on a triggering problem first, a one-sentence solution second, and a low-pressure CTA that sells interest in a meeting rather than the full product.

- The video stresses that tone, confidence, and delivery often matter more than perfect wording.

- For objections, they explain the “Mr. Miyagi method”: agree first, invite conversation, and then guide the prospect toward a low-pressure next step.

- The live-call examples reinforce that strong outbound is about reducing pressure, learning through the objection, and selling the “test drive” rather than forcing a close on the call.

Key Insights
- Great cold calls start with relevance and tone, not scripts that sound like every other seller.

- Problem-led messaging beats feature-led pitching because it gives the solution context.

- Objection handling works best when the rep lowers resistance before trying to redirect the conversation.

2) Cold Calling Masterclass: 52 Minutes of Proven Cold Calling Tips (For B2B Sales)

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gtKZ7fP1HZM
 
Date: Jul 18, 2024 

Transcript Summary
- This masterclass is structured as a full cold-calling playbook covering whether cold calling still works, how to open, how to pitch, and how to handle objections, gatekeepers, and voicemail.

- The session emphasizes that the first minute of a cold call determines whether the seller gets placed in the telemarketer bucket or earns real attention.

- It teaches that effective value propositions on cold calls should be rooted in specific buyer problems rather than vague category language or generic product claims.

- The video also addresses the off-phone side of outbound, including how reps can improve connect rates, increase set rates, and make dial blocks more efficient.

- Nick and Armand treat cold calling as part of a broader outbound operating system, where consistency, repetition, and process discipline matter as much as the call script itself.

- The included timestamp structure makes it especially useful as a practical reference for building a repeatable outbound training system for SDRs and AEs.

Key Insights
- Cold calling still has value when reps sound different from the market and lead with buyer context.

- The best call frameworks combine messaging skill with operational discipline off the phones.

- This video is especially valuable for teams building repeatable training around outbound fundamentals.
