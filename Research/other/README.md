## Blog Posts

**Jeremy Chatelaine — QuickMail / MonsterOps**
Two standalone articles (not LinkedIn posts) already summarized in `research/linkedin-posts/jeremy-chatelaine.md` as Posts 4 and 5. Listed here as well since they're blog-native content, not LinkedIn-native:

- "Cold Email Deliverability Is Not The Problem" (~March 24, 2025)
  https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/cold-email-deliverability-not-problem-jeremy-chatelaine-
  Argues low reply rates signal an offer problem before they signal a deliverability problem — a theme echoed independently by Eric Nowoslawski and Nick Abraham (see `research/insights.md`, Section 3: "Offer Quality Is More Important Than Copy").

- "Deliverability: The Next Evolution" (~May 26, 2025)
  https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/deliverability-next-evolution-quickmail-innovation-jeremy-chatelaine
  Covers QuickMail's Stealth mode and the case for continuous deliverability innovation over legacy tooling.
