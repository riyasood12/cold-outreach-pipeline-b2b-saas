## Post 1
Date: November 22, 2022

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/morganjingram_sales-coldcalling-activity-7000821192991412224-oDMe

Summary: Introduces a 10/30/10 LinkedIn voice note framework: 10 seconds for the reason you're reaching out (job change, LinkedIn post, website trigger), 30 seconds for a value prop tied to that specific scenario or pain point, 10 seconds for a frictionless interest-based CTA such as "Would you be interested in learning more?" rather than a meeting request.

Key Takeaway: Structuring a voice note around a named trigger converts it from a cold interruption into a relevant, reply-worthy message.

## Cross-Cutting Themes

Human signal in an AI-saturated inbox — Posts 2, 4, and 5 all converge on the same 2025 thesis: AI has made generic outbound cheap and ubiquitous, which means genuine relevance is now the actual competitive moat, not tooling or volume
Trigger-first, then message — Posts 1, 3, and 5 all share the same underlying principle: identify a specific, named reason to reach out before choosing the channel or writing the message
