# YouTube Transcripts

This folder contains transcript-based summaries of long-form videos, podcast appearances, and outbound sales interviews from selected B2B SaaS cold outreach practitioners.

Files are organized by expert.

Each file includes:

* Video title
* Source URL
* Publication date
* Transcript summary
* Key insights
* Rationale for video selection

## Research Scope

While LinkedIn research was completed for all 10 experts in this project, transcript-based YouTube and podcast research was focused on experts with the strongest availability of long-form educational content.

The experts currently covered in this folder are:

* Eric Nowoslawski
* Nick Abraham
* Jason Bay
* Josh Braun
* Morgan J. Ingram
* 30MPC (Nick Cegelski & Armand Farrokh)

## Methodology

Video transcripts were collected using the `youtube-transcript-api` Python library where transcripts were available.

Transcript content was reviewed and summarized to identify recurring themes across:

* Prospecting
* Messaging
* Deliverability
* Infrastructure
* AI-assisted workflows
* Cold calling
* Multichannel outbound execution

The goal is not to document every detail from each video, but to capture the highest-signal ideas that repeatedly appear across experienced outbound practitioners.

These transcript-based summaries support the synthesized findings documented in `research/insights.md`.
