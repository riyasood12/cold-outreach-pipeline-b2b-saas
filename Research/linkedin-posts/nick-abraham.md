## Cross-Cutting Themes
Theme 1: Fundamentals (list quality, deliverability, offer strength) outperform tactical tweaks like personalization and copy changes
Theme 2: Channel scarcity creates opportunity—underused channels (InMail) and underused signals (prior response behavior) deliver outsized results
Theme 3: Overplayed tactics (fake "Re:", standard copy templates) degrade faster than ever—differentiation must come from the offer, not the format
