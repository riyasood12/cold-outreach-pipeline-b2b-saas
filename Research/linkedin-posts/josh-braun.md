# Josh Braun

## Post 1

Date: ~June 12, 2026 (6 days before compile date)

URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/josh-braun_one-of-the-most-useful-sales-lessons-doesn-activity-7470865129073520640-BsoU

Summary: Braun applies psychologist George Loewenstein’s “information gap” theory to sales. Most sellers over-explain and pitch; curiosity appears when prospects notice a gap between what they know and what they want to know. He contrasts a feature-heavy pitch with a neutral question about donor conversion rates.

Key Takeaway: Don’t fill information gaps, expose them. Ask questions that make prospects look inward and discover gaps on their own; curiosity beats convincing.

Category: Messaging psychology
